Our Collection Of Oahu's Best Restaurants !!

  • Pa'akai

    • Our ocean-to-table evening dining experience at Pa'akai showcases Hawaii’s fresh seafood with a mix-and-match approach to sauces, side dishes and cooking styles. Proud recipient of the TripAdvisor Excellence Award, and Open Table Diners' Choice.

  • Roy's Beach House

    • Located right on breathtaking Bay View Beach, this Oahu restaurant by renowned Chef Roy Yamaguchi is a casual yet upscale alternative for resort guests, residents, and visitors. Dine-in at this North Shore restaurant with amazing service and views or swing by the take-out counter for some delicious options to enjoy on the beach!
